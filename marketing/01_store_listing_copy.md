## ROTATION SCHEDULE

App Store algorithms reward freshness. Update these fields on a schedule:
- **Promotional Text** → every 2 weeks (no review required on iOS)
- **Screenshots** → every 8 weeks or with each major feature
- **Keywords** → every 12 weeks, swap underperformers
- **Description** → every 12 weeks, rewrite top 3 lines

Track which rotations lifted installs in your Play Console / App Store Connect analytics.
