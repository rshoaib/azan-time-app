# Screenshot Design Brief

> Screenshots drive ~80% of install decisions. Most prayer apps ship with cluttered, unreadable-at-thumbnail screenshots. This is your biggest conversion lever.

---

## Design Rules (non-negotiable)

1. **Headline text must be readable at thumbnail size** — 72pt+ font weight bold. Test at 200×400px.
2. **One idea per screenshot.** Never put 2 features on one slide.
3. **Brand color (Gold `#D4AF37` + Navy `#1A2C6B`) on every screen.** Instant recognition in search results.
4. **Show the app chrome + your feature, not just a marketing collage.** Users want to know it's real.
5. **Localize all 5 screenshots for each language you add to the store.** Translated screenshots alone can lift install conversion 15–25%.
6. **Portrait only.** Landscape is dead on mobile stores.

## Required Resolutions

### iOS (App Store Connect requirements)
- iPhone 6.9" (iPhone 16 Pro Max): **1290 × 2796** px — REQUIRED
- iPhone 6.5" (iPhone 11 Pro Max): **1242 × 2688** px — REQUIRED
- iPad 12.9" (if you want tablet visibility): **2048 × 2732** px — optional but boost

### Android (Play Store)
- Minimum 2 screenshots required, up to 8
- Phone: minimum **320px** on short side, max **3840px** — aim for **1080 × 1920** or **1440 × 2560**
- Ratio: 16:9 or 9:16
- 7-inch tablet + 10-inch tablet screenshots also indexed — ship all three

---

## Screenshot A/B Testing (Play Console — free)

Play Console supports free store listing experiments. Test, in this order:

1. **First screenshot's headline** — biggest impact
2. **Icon variant** — 5%+ CTR lift is common
3. **Screenshot order** — move different value props to slot 1

Run each experiment at least 7 days with statistical significance before calling a winner.

## Fonts + Colors (brand lock)

**Primary font:** Inter Bold (free from Google Fonts) — legible at thumbnail size
**Arabic font:** Amiri (free from Google Fonts) — for "بسم الله" accent

**Color palette:**
- Gold: `#D4AF37` — primary accent, headlines
- Navy: `#1A2C6B` — backgrounds, hero cards
- Emerald: `#0D9488` — success states, tracker
- Cream: `#F0EDE6` — ayah cards, warm screens
- Off-white: `#F5F6FA` — light backgrounds

These match your app's Theme.colors, so screenshots will feel continuous with the product.
